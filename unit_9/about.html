S/KO PROJECTS

Susanna Ko is a creative director based in New York City. Susanna thrives on evolving and establishing creative teams, strategy, process and developing visual DNA for entrepreneurially spirited companies. Susanna is currently the Creative Director of Galison Mudpuppy, a publisher of stationery, puzzles and gifts for ages 1-101. Prior to that, she served as the Creative Director of Product Development at Bloom Stationers, initiating and managing all aspects of product design development for the startup, overseeing a team of eighteen talents, while partnering with web developers and vendors.

For nine years, Susanna was a Design Director at Martha Stewart, overseeing all aspects of visual expression for brand creative plus development of new product design programs for the retail merchandising department. She began her career in strategic creative for lifestyle brands at Slover and Company, with clients including Donghia, Chronicle Books and Nordstrom Product Group.

Susanna has taught branding and typography classes at Parsons School of Design. A native of California, Susanna graduated from Parsons School of Design with a BFA in Painting and Illustration. Always evolving a career founded on the love of multidisciplinary art and design, Susanna has a secondary degree in Fashion Business Management from the Fashion Institute of Technology New York City. She continues to pursue studies in technology, design and crafts at General Assembly and BKLYN CLAY.
