MoMA: The Museum of Modern Art

Founded in 1929, The Museum of Modern Art in New York is the foremost museum of modern art in the world. The Museum's evolving collection contains almost 200,000 works of modern and contemporary art, ranging from the late 19th century to the present. These product designs feature details from works by artists represented in the MoMA collection.
